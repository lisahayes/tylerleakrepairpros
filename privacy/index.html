Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Tyler Leak Repair Experts handles information when you use this website or call us. We keep it short because we collect very little.

What we collect

This site has no contact form, no newsletter signup, and no account login. When you call (903) 651-5125, your phone provides your number to us the same way any phone call does, and our call routing records standard call details so we can return your call and improve service. We do not ask for payment information through the website.

Call tracking

Calls placed through this site may be routed and recorded through a third-party call service so we can connect you with a licensed plumber and review service quality. Standard details such as the time of the call, its duration, and the number you called from may be logged. Reach us anytime at (903) 651-5125.

Website analytics

We may use basic analytics to understand which pages people read and how they found us. That data is aggregated and is not used to identify you personally.

Cookies

The site may set basic cookies needed for it to function and for simple analytics. You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings without losing access to the content.

Your choices

Because we do not collect personal profiles through this site, there is little stored to request or delete. If you have a question about a call you placed to us, call (903) 651-5125 and we will help.

Updates

We may revise this policy as the site changes. The current version always lives on this page.
